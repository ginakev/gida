About GIDA

Founded in 2020

Ginakev Digital Academy (GIDA) is a Crypto Academy that specializes on Crypto Education, providing insightful trading education and awareness on emerging digital currencies for optimum profits.

Learn Before Earning – A popular slogan in the cryptosphere.

GIDA provides persons with quality education and enlightenment to acquire and perfect the trading Skill, as well as new digital wealth creation through Cryptocurrency, Blockchain and its various facets.

Our Mission and Core Values

The primary vision and goal of GIDA is to empower individuals with financial education, empower them to be financially free, and capable to live the life they desire.

In four words, “Financial Inclusion For All”.

About Kevin

Popularly known as “Ezemmuo Blockchain”, Okoye Kevin Chibuoyim is the Founder of Ginakev Digital Academy – A cryptocurrency education platform that focuses on cryptocurrency awareness, education and benefits.

Kevin is an avid Cryptocurrency Enthusiast and Entrepreneur. Since his encounter with Blockchain and Cryptocurrencies, as well as its related trading in 2018, he has always been at the forefront to drive Crypto adoption in Nigeria/Africa through proper education and enlightenment, devoid of hype. He has successfully trained over 2000+ individuals from Africa and exposed them to the world of cryptocurrencies via free/paid courses since then.

He is equally the Co-founder of BlockchainUNN, a blockchain campus club at the University of Nigeria Nsukka aimed at promoting blockchain adoption and education within the university environment.

He anchored the Mega BlockchainUNN Blockchain Conference, with over 1000+ attendees present physically, the first and premier Blockchain Conference in the South East of Nigeria.

He was recently featured by CryptoTvPlus, a leading Crypto Media Outfit in Africa, in recognition of his works in the Crypto and Blockchain Industry.
